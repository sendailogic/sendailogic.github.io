About the workshop:

The scope of the workshop involves the topics Proof Theory, Computability Theory, Constructive Mathematics, Theory of Randomness and Computational Complexity Theory.
This is a successor workshop to Workshop on Proof Theory and Computability Theory (February 2011, Miyagi, Japan).

This workshop is supported by JSPS-FWO bilateral programs and is held as part of the research project Philosophical Frontiers in Reverse Mathematics sponsored by the John Templeton Foundation.
The latter project aims to develop Reverse Mathematics and Nonstandard Analysis to answer several questions about philosophical aspects of Mathematics.
