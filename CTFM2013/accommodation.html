Accommodation

Ookayama campus of Tokyo Institute of Technology is very close to Ookayama train station, and there are various hotels that have good train connections to Ookayama train station. For this reason participants are kindly encouraged to book their hotels by their own.
A list of these hotels can be found below.
Rakuten Travel online hotel booking system may also help.

Hotels

Tokyo Prince Hotel

Basic rate: JPY 6,250 per night around JPY 13,000 per night.
Direct bus service from Narita airport (JPY 3,000) and Haneda airport (JPY 900). 20 minutes to Ookayama Station.

Ours Inn Hankyu

Basic rate: JPY 5,500 per night.
Direct bus service from Haneda airport (JPY 560). 10 minutes to Ookayama Station.

HOTEL ROUTE-INN Shinagawa Oimachi

Basic rate: around JPY 7,000 per night including breakfasts.
15 minutes to Ookayama Station.
