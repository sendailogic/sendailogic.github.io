Accommodation

Ookayama campus of Tokyo Institute of Technology is very close to Ookayama train station. There are various hotels that have good train connections to Ookayama train station. Participants are kindly encouraged to book their hotels on their own.
A list of these hotels can be found below.

Rakuten Travel online hotel booking system may help.

Hotels

  • Richmond Hotel Premier Musashikosugi

    • Narita Express from Narita airport (JPY 3,360). Direct bus service from Haneda airport (JPY 920). 10 minutes to Ookayama Station.

  • Tokyo Prince Hotel

    • Direct bus service from Narita airport (JPY 3,100). Tokyo Monorail from Haneda airport. 20 minutes to Ookayama Station.

  • Ours Inn Hankyu

    • Direct bus service from Haneda airport (JPY 580). 10 minutes to Ookayama Station.

  • HOTEL ROUTE-INN Shinagawa Oimachi

    • 15 minutes to Ookayama Station.

  • Hotel Abest Megro

  • Tokyo Grand Hotel

  • The Prince Park Tower Tokyo

  • Shiba Park Hotel
