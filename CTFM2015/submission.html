Submission for presentations at CTFM

Deadline for Presentations

June 15, 2015. June 29, 2015.

Submitting for Presentations

Abstracts of contributed talks have to be submitted via the EasyChair submission for CTFM 2015. The length of every abstract is limited to 2 pages including references. The authors are recommended to prepare their abstracts in the Springer LNCS format [llncs2e.zip]. For more information, please visit Information for LNCS Authors.
The abstract must contain Title, Author(s), E-mail address and Institute.
Abstracts of accepted presentations will be made available on the timetable page of CTFM.

Submitted abstracts will be reviewed on arrival.
