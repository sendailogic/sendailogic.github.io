Submission for presentations at CTFM

Deadline for submission of presentations

August 31, 2016.

Submitting for Presentations

Abstracts of contributed talks should be submitted via email to ctfm2016[at]fastmail.com with subject line: CTFM2016 submission.

The length of every abstract is limited to 2 pages including references. The authors are recommended to prepare their abstracts in the Springer LNCS format [llncs2e.zip] and requested to send both pdf and latex source files. For more information, please visit Information for LNCS Authors. The abstract must contain Title, Author(s), E-mail address and Institute. Abstracts of accepted presentations will be made available on the timetable page of CTFM.

Submitted abstracts will be reviewed on arrival.
