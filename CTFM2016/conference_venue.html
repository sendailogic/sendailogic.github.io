Conference Venue

CTFM will take place on the third floor of the International Conference Center (number 18 on the map below) of Waseda University. The center is connected to the Central Library (unlike the International center, number 22 on the map).
Address: 1-20-14, Nishiwaseda, Shinjyuku-ku, Tokyo, Japan.

Participants staying at the Sunshine City Prince Hotel Ikebukuro can join the guided tour to the conference venue, which starts 9:00 on the 20th, in the reception area of the hotel.

Banquet

The banquet will be held on September 20 (18:30-20:30), at the same place as the conference venue. Please register before September 12 if you wish to attend the banquet.
